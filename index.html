Privacy Policy for Nuzzle It

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Nuzzle It ("we", "our", or "the app") is a puzzle game developed by Carte Blanche. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Device ID: A randomly generated identifier stored on your device. This is used to save your progress and submit your scores to the global leaderboard.
  • Nickname: A display name you choose voluntarily for the leaderboard.
  • Puzzle scores: Your move count per level, submitted to our leaderboard database.
  • Purchase information: In-app purchases are handled by Google Play. We do not store your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To display your scores on the global leaderboard
  • To provide in-app purchase functionality

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: We use Supabase to store leaderboard scores. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play: In-app purchases are processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are retained indefinitely. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Nuzzle It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
